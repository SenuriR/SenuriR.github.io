Perturbation Theory Visualizer

Interactive exploration of quantum perturbation theory using the anharmonic oscillator.

Overview

I am currently taking Quantum Mechanics II, and we are learning about perturbation theory. I wanted to create a visualizer to help me understand the concepts better. I realized that creating an application that was centered on the idea that lambda is a tunable parameter would be a great way to explore the concepts.

Why lambda

The idea about perturbation theory is that you have this nice Hamiltonian that you know how to solve exactly, but it's not the Hamiltonian you're interested in.

The Hamiltonian you're interested in is this simple Hamiltonian PLUS a perturbation term that you can't solve exactly. So you try to solve it by adding a small parameter lambda to this perturbation term.

We can then expand the hamiltonian in terms of lambda and solve for the wavefunctions and energies order by order.

What I’m Learning

Quantum mechanics can be very abstact, but tools like this that allow you to interact with the parameters and concepts at play help you understand it better.
